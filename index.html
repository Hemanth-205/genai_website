Some common questions
were often asked

What is generative AI?

Generative AI refers to algorithms that can create new content, such as text, images, and music, based on patterns learned from existing data

How does generative AI work?

It uses deep learning models, often trained on large datasets, to generate new outputs by predicting the next element in a sequence or creating novel combinations.

What are the applications of generative AI?

Applications include content creation, art generation, game development, drug discovery, and even personalized marketing.

Is generative AI safe to use?

While it has many benefits, it also raises concerns around misinformation, copyright issues, and ethical use, necessitating responsible implementation.

Can generative AI replace human creativity?

Generative AI can assist and augment human creativity but lacks the emotional depth and context that human creators bring to their work.

How do I get started with generative AI?

Beginners can start by exploring online tutorials, using pre-built models on platforms like TensorFlow or PyTorch, and experimenting with applications like ChatGPT or DALL-E.
